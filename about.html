About Our Stationery Inventory Management System

The Stationery Inventory Management System is a web-based platform that helps manage office and stationery supplies efficiently. It integrates supplier and product data, allowing users to track stock levels, manage suppliers, and generate reports through a simple interface powered by Flask and MySQL.

Objectives

  • Maintain accurate supplier and product information.
  • Ensure smooth communication between inventory modules.
  • Provide easy-to-use features for managing data and reports.

Team Roles

  • Member 1 – Product Management: Handles product creation, updates, and linking products to suppliers.
  • Member 2 – Stationery Supplier Data Management: Manages supplier records and connects suppliers with their products.
  • Member 3 – User & Authentication: Develops secure login and role-based access for system users.
  • Member 4 – Reporting & Analytics: Generates supplier and product reports with useful insights.

Summary

Together, these modules create a complete stationery management system that supports efficient tracking, secure access, and data-driven decisions for inventory management.
